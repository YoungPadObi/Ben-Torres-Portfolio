Bio

Born and raised in the Bronx, I left the big city at 25 to work for Disney Cruise Line. While working for DCL, I worked with and fell in love with my wife. We've spent the last five years on a nomadic adventure around the United States and wound up in Sarasota, Florida.

When we moved here, I began working in the electronic repair industry, which rekindled my interest in tech. I enjoyed working on the hardware side, but I have always idolized the ability of engineers like Geordi La Forge, Harry Kim, and B'Elanna Torres to solve ANY kind of problem, both hardware and software. Characters though they may be, I admire their dedication to problem-solving; that's why I enjoy coding. It's been quite the journey teaching myself to code. I started on freecodecamp.com, which I HIGHLY recommend, and have found my way to Thinkful. If you want to find out more of what I'm about, check me out at any of my handles below.
